Waste-Co Services:

Users can sign up and schedule pickups according to their convenience. We utilize advanced algorithms to optimize collection routes, ensuring efficient and timely service to our customers. Our platform also allows users to track the status of their pickups in real-time, providing transparency and peace of mind..

We offer incentives to encourage responsible waste disposal practices and promote environmental sustainability. Users can earn incentives in the form of discounts, rewards points, or even cashback for participating in our recycling programs, reducing waste generation, or referring friends to our service.

To be eligible for incentives, users must adhere to certain guidelines and fulfill specific criteria:
1. Participate in our recycling programs by segregating recyclable materials from general waste.
2. Gather atleast 1kg of recyclable waste
3. Once weighed at our facility, your recyclables will earn you points redeemable for various rewards through your online account

We use clear criteria to analyze your recycling activity and award points based on your contribution. Regularly, we review our system to ensure it stays fair and helps us achieve our environmental goals together.
